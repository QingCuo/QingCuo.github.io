About the Series

Gone with the Wind (轻错 Qīng Cuò lit. Tender Mistakes) is a music-themed Chinese BL series by Dr. Solo. The story revolves around the meteoric rise and eventual fallout of the vocalist-guitarist duo of an underground rock band — West Wind.

The novel was serialized from 2018-2019 on the Chinese Web Novel website CPWXW (长佩文学网) and is completed at 99 chapters. A comic adaptation is currently being serialized weekly on Bilibili Comics.
