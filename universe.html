Soul Vibration (2017)

灵魂骚动

Soul Vibration tells the daily lives of the members of LOTUS, a chart-topping rock band, told from the perspective of the Bassist's personal assistant.

The novel has been licensed officially by Via Lactea for digital and physical release in simplified Chinese and English. A comic adaptation has been confirmed for serialization on Bilibili Comics.
